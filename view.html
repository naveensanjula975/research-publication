Multidecadal Land Use Patterns and Land Surface Temperature Variation in Sri Lanka

Editor in Chief: Dr. Mahesh Hapugoda

Agricultural land conversion due to urbanization, industrialization, and many other factors is one of the significant concerns to food production. This study aims to explore the multidecadal changes in land use patterns and how these changes have impacted land surface temperature in Sri Lanka.
